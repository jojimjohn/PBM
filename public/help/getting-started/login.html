Login & Authentication

Learn how to securely access the PBM system, including company selection, password management, and session security features.

Logging Into the System

The PBM system uses a secure login process with company selection to ensure you access the correct business data.

  1. Navigate to the Login Page

    Open your web browser and go to the PBM system URL provided by your administrator. You'll see the login page with the Al Ramrami Trading logo.

  2. Select Your Company

    Before entering your credentials, select the company you want to access from the dropdown menu:

    • Al Ramrami Trading Enterprises - Oil and petroleum business
    • Pride Muscat International LLC - Scrap materials business
    Important

    Selecting the correct company is crucial. Each company has separate data, and you can only view information for the company you select.

  3. Enter Your Credentials

    Enter your login credentials:

    • Email: Your work email address (e.g., john@alramrami.com)
    • Password: Your secure password

    Passwords are case-sensitive. Make sure Caps Lock is not accidentally enabled.

  4. Click "Sign In"

    Click the Sign In button to authenticate. If your credentials are correct, you'll be redirected to your dashboard.

Session Security & Timeout

For security purposes, your session will automatically expire after a period of inactivity.

How Session Timeout Works

  • Default Timeout: 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Activity Tracking: Actions like clicking, typing, and submitting forms reset the timer
  • Warning Notification: You'll see a warning 5 minutes before your session expires
  • Extend Session: Click "Stay Logged In" in the warning to extend your session
Security Notice

If you leave your workstation, always log out manually or lock your computer. Don't rely solely on session timeout for security.

Password Management

Password Requirements

For security, passwords must meet these requirements:

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • At least one number (0-9)
  • At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

Changing Your Password

  1. Access Settings

    Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.

  2. Navigate to Security

    In the Settings page, find the "Security" or "Password" section.

  3. Enter Passwords

    Enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password.

  4. Save Changes

    Click "Update Password" to save. You may be asked to log in again with your new password.

Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password, contact your system administrator. They can reset your password and provide you with temporary credentials.

Logging Out

Always log out when you're done using the system, especially on shared computers.

  1. Click Your Profile

    Click on your name or profile icon in the top-right corner of any page.

  2. Select "Logout"

    Click the "Logout" option from the dropdown menu. You'll be redirected to the login page.

Security Best Practice

If using a shared or public computer, clear your browser history and close all browser windows after logging out.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Problem Possible Cause Solution
"Invalid credentials" error Incorrect email or password Double-check your email and password. Remember passwords are case-sensitive.
Can't see expected data after login Wrong company selected Log out and log back in, selecting the correct company.
Session keeps timing out Extended inactivity The system times out after 30 minutes of inactivity. Stay active or click "Stay Logged In" when warned.
Account locked Too many failed login attempts Contact your administrator to unlock your account.
Page won't load Network or server issue Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page or contact IT support.
