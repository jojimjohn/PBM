Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is a workflow-based hub for monitoring business activities, tracking tasks, and quickly accessing all modules. This guide explains each section of the dashboard.

Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll land on your personalized dashboard. The dashboard is organized into five main sections designed to give you a complete overview of your work and business status.

Notifications Panel

The Notifications Panel appears at the top of your dashboard and shows important alerts requiring your attention.

  • Action Required: Items needing immediate attention (approvals, overdue tasks)
  • System Alerts: Important system messages and updates
  • Reminders: Upcoming deadlines and scheduled items
Notification Badge

A badge on the notification icon indicates unread alerts. Click to expand and view all notifications.

Key Metrics

These cards show your most important business metrics at a glance:

Collections

Number of active and pending material collections from suppliers.

Purchase Orders

Count of purchase orders and their current status.

Invoices

Invoice status including pending, paid, and overdue counts.

Pending Actions

Items requiring your attention or approval.

Module Overview

Quick access to all modules with their current status. Each module card shows a summary of activity within that module.

  • Click any module card to navigate directly to that module
  • Each card displays a status indicator showing recent activity
  • Module availability depends on your role and permissions
Quick Navigation

The Module Overview provides the fastest way to jump to any section of the system without using the sidebar menu.

Your Tasks

View and manage your pending tasks organized by priority. This section helps you stay on top of your workload.

Priority Level Description
High Priority Urgent tasks that appear first - approvals, overdue items, critical actions
Normal Priority Standard tasks with no immediate deadline

Click on any task to view details or take action directly from the dashboard.

Recent Activity

Track recent actions across the system. This activity feed shows what's happening in real-time across all modules you have access to.

  • Shows actions from all users in your company
  • Includes order creation, approvals, status changes, and more
  • Click any activity item to see full details
  • Filter by activity type or time period

Using the Dashboard

The dashboard is interactive and designed for quick access:

  • Click metric cards: Navigate to related modules for details
  • Click module cards: Jump directly to any module
  • Click tasks: Open task details and take action
  • Click activity items: View the full record
  • Refresh: Data updates automatically, or click refresh to manually update
