Petty Cash System

Manage petty cash cards, track expenses, and process approvals. The Petty Cash module provides complete control over small cash expenditures across your organization.

Who Can Access

Full access requires Accounts Staff or Admin. Staff can view and submit expenses. Managers can approve expenses.

System Overview

The Petty Cash system has four main components:

Petty Cash Cards

Virtual cards assigned to departments or individuals with set limits and balances.

PC Users

Staff members assigned to cards who can submit expenses through the mobile portal.

Expenses

Individual expense records with receipts, categories, and approval status.

Quick Actions

Approve or reject expenses directly from the Expenses tab using action buttons.

Managing Petty Cash Cards

Navigate to Finance â†’ Petty Cash to access the Cards tab.

Creating a Card

  1. Click Add Card

    Click + Add Card in the Cards tab.

  2. Enter Card Details
    • Card Name * - Descriptive name (e.g., "Operations Petty Cash")
    • Card Number * - Unique identifier
    • Assigned To - Primary user responsible for the card
    • Department - Department using this card
    • Initial Balance * - Starting amount on the card
    • Monthly Limit - Maximum monthly spending (optional)
  3. Save Card

    Click Save. The card is now active and ready for expenses.

Card Statuses

Status Description
Active Card can be used for expenses
Suspended Temporarily disabled - no new expenses allowed
Expired Card has passed its validity period
Closed Card is permanently deactivated

Managing PC Users

PC Users are staff members who can submit expenses through the mobile portal.

Adding a PC User

  1. Go to Users Tab

    In the Petty Cash page, click the Users tab.

  2. Click Add User

    Click + Add PC User.

  3. Enter User Details
    • Name * - User's full name
    • Email - Contact email
    • Phone * - Mobile number (for portal access)
    • Assigned Card * - Which petty cash card they use
  4. Generate Access Credentials

    The system generates a QR code and PIN for portal access. Share these with the user securely.

Mobile Portal

PC Users access the system through a special mobile-friendly portal using QR + PIN. See the Mobile Portal Guide.

Managing Expenses

The Expenses tab shows all expense submissions across all cards.

Submitting an Expense (Admin)

  1. Click Add Expense

    In the Expenses tab, click + Add Expense.

  2. Select Card

    Choose which petty cash card to charge.

  3. Enter Expense Details
    • Amount * - Expense amount
    • Category * - Select from predefined categories
    • Description * - Brief explanation of the expense
    • Date - When the expense occurred
    • PC User - Optionally assign to a specific user
  4. Attach Receipt

    Upload a photo or scan of the receipt (recommended for audit trail).

  5. Submit

    Click Submit. The expense goes to pending approval status.

Expense Categories

  • Transportation / Fuel
  • Office Supplies
  • Meals & Entertainment
  • Maintenance & Repairs
  • Utilities
  • Miscellaneous

Expense Approval Process

Expenses require manager approval before being finalized. All approvals are handled within the Expenses tab using quick actions.

For Managers: Approving Expenses

  1. View Pending Expenses

    In the Expenses tab, filter by "Pending" status to see expenses awaiting approval.

  2. Review Expense Details

    Click on an expense to view details, receipt, and submitter information.

  3. Use Quick Actions

    Each pending expense has quick action buttons:

    • Approve: Click the approve button - card balance is deducted
    • Reject: Click the reject button - provide a reason for rejection

Expense Statuses

Status Description
Pending Awaiting manager approval
Approved Approved and deducted from card balance
Rejected Rejected by manager with reason

Card Operations

Reloading a Card

When a card's balance runs low:

  1. Click on the card to open details
  2. Click Reload Card
  3. Enter the amount to add
  4. Add a note explaining the reload
  5. Click Confirm

Suspending a Card

To temporarily disable a card:

  1. Click on the card
  2. Click Suspend Card
  3. The card can be reactivated later

Viewing Card History

Each card has a complete transaction history showing all expenses, reloads, and balance changes.

Best Practices

  • Require receipts: Enforce receipt uploads for all expenses
  • Set appropriate limits: Configure monthly limits based on department needs
  • Regular reconciliation: Compare card balances with physical cash periodically
  • Timely approvals: Process pending approvals within 24-48 hours
  • Review rejected expenses: Follow up on rejected items with submitters
  • Monitor balances: Reload cards before they run out completely
