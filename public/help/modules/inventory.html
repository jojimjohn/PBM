Inventory Management

Track materials, monitor stock levels, and manage inventory movements. The Inventory module provides real-time visibility into your oil products and ensures you never run out of stock.

Who Can Access

Inventory is viewable by All Staff. Edit permissions require Manager or Admin role.

Materials in PBM

Al Ramrami Trading deals with various petroleum products. Common materials include:

Oil Products

  • Engine Oil
  • Transformer Oil
  • Lubricant Oil
  • Cooking Oil
  • Diesel

Containers

  • Empty Drums

Composite Materials

Some materials auto-split during collection:

  • "Engine Oil with Drums" → Engine Oil + Empty Drums

Inventory Dashboard

Navigate to Inventory → Stock to view your inventory dashboard.

Stock Level Indicators

Color Status Description
Healthy Stock above reorder point
Low Stock at or below reorder point
Critical Stock below minimum threshold
Out of Stock Zero quantity available

Viewing Stock Levels

The inventory list shows all materials with their current quantities:

Column Description
Material Code Unique identifier (e.g., MAT-001)
Material Name Product description
Category Product category (Oil, Container, etc.)
Current Stock Available quantity
Unit Measurement unit (Liters, Pieces, etc.)
Reorder Point Level triggering reorder alert
Market Price Current selling price per unit

Filtering Stock

  • By Category: Filter to show only specific product types
  • By Status: Show only low stock or out of stock items
  • Search: Find specific materials by name or code

Stock Movements

Stock levels change through various transactions:

Stock In (Increases)

  • WCN Finalization (collections)
  • Purchase Order receipt
  • Manual adjustment

Stock Out (Decreases)

  • Sales orders
  • Wastage records
  • Manual adjustment

Movement History

To view movement history for a material:

  1. Click on the material row
  2. Go to the History or Movements tab
  3. View all transactions affecting this material

Material Details

Click on any material to view its complete information:

Material Profile Includes:

  • Basic Info: Name, code, category, unit of measure
  • Pricing: Current market price, cost price
  • Stock Info: Current level, minimum, reorder point
  • Movement History: All stock changes with dates
  • Related Orders: Recent sales and purchases

Low Stock Alerts

The system automatically alerts you when stock reaches critical levels:

  1. Dashboard Widget

    Low stock items appear on the main dashboard with red/yellow indicators.

  2. Notifications

    Bell icon shows alerts for items below reorder point.

  3. Take Action

    Create purchase orders or schedule collections to replenish stock.

Proactive Management

Review the Low Stock report weekly to plan collections and avoid stockouts.

Stock Visualization

The inventory dashboard includes visual charts showing:

  • Stock Distribution: Pie chart of stock by category
  • Stock Trends: Line chart showing level changes over time
  • Value Summary: Total inventory value by category

Best Practices

  • Regular reviews: Check stock levels daily
  • Set appropriate thresholds: Configure reorder points based on lead times
  • Document wastage: Record all losses through the Wastage module
  • Verify movements: Cross-check physical counts periodically
  • Plan collections: Schedule based on stock needs and supplier availability
