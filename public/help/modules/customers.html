Customer Management

Manage your customer database including contact information, credit settings, and customer history.

Module Availability

This module is available for Al Ramrami Trading Enterprises (Oil Trading) only.

Accessing Customers

Navigate to Customers from the main sidebar menu. This is a top-level navigation item appearing after Dashboard.

Customer List

The customer list displays all customers with the following columns:

Column Description
Customer Customer name and code with avatar icon
Contact Person Primary contact name
Phone Contact phone number
Email Contact email address
Outstanding Balance Current amount owed by customer
Credit Status Status badge (Good, Warning, On Hold)
Actions View, Edit, Create Order, View Contract, Delete

Viewing Customer Details

Click the View button or customer row to open the details view. The customer details are organized into sections:

Basic Information

  • Customer Name
  • Customer Code
  • Customer Type
  • Status (Active/Inactive)

Contact Information

  • Contact Person
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Address
  • City/Region

Business Terms

  • Credit Limit (OMR)
  • Payment Terms (days)
  • Outstanding Balance
  • Credit Status

Adding a New Customer

  1. Click Add Customer

    Click the + Add Customer button in the top-right corner.

  2. Enter Customer Information

    Fill in the required fields:

    • Customer Name * - Company or individual name
    • Customer Code - Auto-generated (AR-CUST-XXX)
    • Customer Type * - Select from dropdown
    • Contact Person - Primary contact name
    • Phone * - Contact number
    • Email - Contact email
    • Address - Street address
    • City/Region - Location
  3. Set Credit Terms
    • Credit Limit - Maximum credit allowed (OMR)
    • Payment Terms - Days allowed for payment
  4. Add Notes (Optional)

    Add any additional notes about the customer.

  5. Save

    Click Save to create the customer record.

Editing a Customer

  1. Find the customer in the list
  2. Click the Edit button (pencil icon)
  3. Modify the required fields
  4. Click Save to update

Quick Actions

Create Order

Click Create Order to navigate directly to Sales with this customer pre-selected. The customer is stored temporarily and auto-filled in the sales order form.

View Contract

Click View Contract to see the customer's contract details (if they have an active contract).

Deleting a Customer

  1. Click the Delete button (trash icon)
  2. Confirm the deletion in the popup
Note

Customers with existing orders or transactions cannot be deleted. Consider marking them as inactive instead.
