Mobile Expense Portal

The Mobile Expense Portal allows PC Users to submit expenses from their phone without needing full system access. Use QR code + PIN authentication for quick, secure expense submission.

Who Uses This

This guide is for PC Users - staff members assigned to petty cash cards who submit expenses on the go.

Getting Started

Before you can use the portal, you need to be set up as a PC User by your administrator.

What You'll Receive

  • QR Code: Unique code for scanning to access the portal
  • 4-Digit PIN: Personal identification number for authentication
  • Portal URL: Direct link to the expense portal
Keep Your PIN Secret

Never share your PIN with anyone. If you suspect your PIN is compromised, contact your administrator immediately.

Accessing the Portal

You have two ways to access the expense portal:

Option 1: Scan QR Code

  1. Open your phone's camera
  2. Point at the QR code you received
  3. Tap the notification to open the portal
  4. Enter your 4-digit PIN

Option 2: Direct URL

  1. Open your phone's browser
  2. Go to the portal URL you received
  3. Enter your 4-digit PIN
Bookmark for Quick Access

Save the portal URL to your phone's home screen for quick access anytime.

Portal Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personal dashboard showing:

  • Available Balance: Current balance on your assigned card
  • Recent Expenses: Your recently submitted expenses
  • Pending Approvals: How many of your expenses are awaiting approval
  • Quick Submit Button: Large button to start a new expense submission

Submitting an Expense

Follow these steps to submit an expense from your phone:

  1. Tap "Submit Expense"

    On the dashboard, tap the large "Submit Expense" button.

  2. Enter Amount

    Type the expense amount. Use the numeric keypad provided.

  3. Select Category

    Tap to choose from predefined categories:

    • Transportation / Fuel
    • Office Supplies
    • Meals & Entertainment
    • Maintenance & Repairs
    • Utilities
    • Miscellaneous
  4. Add Description

    Write a brief description of what the expense was for (e.g., "Fuel for delivery truck").

  5. Take Photo of Receipt

    Tap "Add Receipt" and either:

    • Take Photo: Use your camera to capture the receipt
    • Choose from Gallery: Select an existing photo
    Photo Tips

    Ensure the receipt is flat, well-lit, and all text is readable. Include the date and amount on the receipt.

  6. Submit

    Review your entry and tap Submit Expense. You'll see a confirmation message.

Viewing Your Expense History

Tap "My Expenses" to see all your submitted expenses.

Expense Statuses

Status What It Means
Pending Waiting for manager approval
Approved Approved and processed
Rejected Rejected - tap to see the reason

Tap any expense to view its full details, including receipt and approval notes.

Troubleshooting

Problem Solution
"Invalid PIN" error Double-check your PIN. After 3 failed attempts, contact your administrator to reset.
QR code won't scan Ensure good lighting and hold phone steady. Try using the direct URL instead.
Receipt photo won't upload Check your internet connection. Reduce image size if the file is too large.
"Insufficient balance" Your card doesn't have enough funds. Contact accounts staff to reload the card.
Can't see my submitted expense Pull down to refresh the list. Check your internet connection.

Best Practices

  • Submit immediately: Record expenses as they happen to avoid forgetting
  • Keep receipts: Always photograph receipts before they fade
  • Be descriptive: Clear descriptions help faster approvals
  • Choose correct category: Accurate categorization helps accounting
  • Check your balance: Verify available funds before making purchases
  • Monitor approvals: Follow up on pending expenses if not approved within 2 days
