Creating Purchase Orders

This step-by-step guide walks you through creating purchase orders, from the collections workflow for contract suppliers to manual PO creation for one-off purchases.

Required Role

Purchase Staff or higher

Two Ways to Create Purchase Orders

Method 1: Collections Workflow

For contract suppliers - the recommended approach:

  • PO is auto-generated from WCN finalization
  • Contract rates applied automatically
  • Full traceability from callout to payment

Go to Collections Workflow →

Method 2: Manual PO

For non-contract suppliers or one-off purchases:

  • Create PO directly
  • Enter prices manually
  • Requires manager approval

Go to Manual PO Creation →

Method 1: Collections Workflow (Recommended)

For suppliers with contracts, follow this workflow to automatically generate purchase orders.

  1. Step 1: Create a Callout

    Navigate to: Purchase → Callouts

    1. Click + New Callout
    2. Select the Supplier
    3. Select the Collection Location
    4. Set the Scheduled Date/Time
    5. Add any notes about expected materials
    6. Click Save
    Tip

    Schedule callouts based on inventory needs and supplier availability. Check the low stock report first.

  2. Step 2: Execute the Collection

    When the collection occurs, create a Collection Order:

    1. Open the Callout you scheduled
    2. Click Create Collection Order
    3. Enter the actual materials collected
    4. Enter actual quantities (may differ from expected)
    5. Record any issues or notes
    6. Click Save
  3. Step 3: Finalize the WCN

    The Waste Consignment Note (WCN) must be finalized to complete the process:

    1. Open the Collection Order
    2. Review all materials and quantities
    3. Click Finalize WCN
    4. Confirm the action

    What happens automatically:

    • Inventory is updated with collected materials
    • A Purchase Order is generated with contract rates
    • Composite materials are split into components
  4. Step 4: Review the Auto-Generated PO

    Navigate to: Purchase → Purchase Orders

    1. Find the auto-generated PO (source: WCN)
    2. Review materials and quantities
    3. Verify contract rates were applied correctly
    4. PO is ready for processing/payment

Method 2: Manual PO Creation

For non-contract suppliers or one-off purchases:

  1. Step 1: Start New PO

    Navigate to: Purchase → Purchase Orders

    Click + New PO

  2. Step 2: Select Supplier

    Choose the supplier from the dropdown list.

    New Supplier?

    If the supplier doesn't exist, create them first in Supplier Management.

  3. Step 3: Add Materials

    Click + Add Item for each material:

    • Material: Select from material list
    • Quantity: Enter amount to purchase
    • Unit Price: Enter negotiated price

    Add multiple items as needed.

  4. Step 4: Set Terms
    • Payment Terms: Cash, Credit, Net 30, etc.
    • Expected Delivery: When materials will arrive
    • Notes: Special instructions
  5. Step 5: Submit for Approval

    Click Submit to send the PO for manager approval.

    The PO status changes to "Pending Approval".

  6. Step 6: After Approval

    Once approved by manager:

    1. Send PO to supplier
    2. Receive materials when delivered
    3. Update received quantities
    4. Process for payment

Common Issues & Solutions

Issue Solution
Supplier not in dropdown Create the supplier first in Supplier Management
Wrong rates on auto-generated PO Check supplier contract - may be expired or missing location rates
Can't finalize WCN Ensure all required fields are filled in the Collection Order
PO stuck in pending approval Follow up with manager to process approval queue
