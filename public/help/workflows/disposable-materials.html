Using Disposable Materials

Learn how to configure and use disposable materials - items that are collected but automatically converted to wastage instead of being stored in inventory.

Required Role & Estimated Time

Manager or higher to configure | Purchase Staff to use | ~5 minutes to complete

What Are Disposable Materials?

Disposable materials are items that are collected from suppliers but have no value for inventory. When finalized in a WCN, 100% of the collected quantity is automatically converted to wastage.

Common Examples:

Contaminated Materials

Oil mixed with water, sludge, or other contaminants that cannot be processed

Packaging Waste

Empty containers, damaged drums, or packaging materials with no resale value

Water/Sludge

Water separated from oil, bottom sediments, or other non-valuable byproducts

Key Behavior

100% to Wastage - No Inventory

When a disposable material is finalized in a WCN, the entire collected quantity is converted to a wastage record. Nothing goes to inventory. The wastage record is created with Pending status and requires approval.

Step 1: Create a Disposable Material

First, configure a material as disposable in the Material Master.

  1. Navigate to Inventory

    Go to: Inventory ‚Üí Material Master tab

    This tab shows all materials with their type badges (Standard, Composite, Disposable).

  2. Add or Edit Material

    Click + Add Material for a new material, or click the Edit icon on an existing material.

  3. Fill Basic Information
    Field Example
    Material Code DISP-001
    Material Name Contaminated Oil Mixture
    Category Disposable Material
    Unit Liters (L)
  4. Enable Disposable Material

    Scroll down to the Disposable Material section (green-themed). Toggle it to Enabled.

    Note

    Composite materials cannot be marked as disposable. The toggle will be disabled if the material is composite.

  5. Select Default Waste Type

    Choose the waste type that will be applied when this material is finalized:

    Waste Type Use For
    Contamination Mixed or contaminated materials
    Sorting Loss Materials lost during sorting/grading
    Quality Rejection Materials failing quality standards
    Spillage Materials spilled during collection
    Evaporation Volatile materials that evaporated
    Other Any other waste category
  6. Save the Material

    Click Save. The material now appears in the list with a green Disposable badge.

Step 2: Use in a Collection Order

Add the disposable material to a collection order like any other material.

  1. Create or Open Collection Order

    Go to: Purchase ‚Üí Collections tab

    Create a new callout or open an existing collection order.

  2. Add the Disposable Material

    In the materials section, add the disposable material with the quantity collected.

    Example: Oil Sludge - 50 liters

  3. Complete the Collection

    Progress the collection through the normal workflow:

    • Assign driver
    • Update status to In Progress ‚Üí Completed
    • Enter actual quantities collected

Step 3: Finalize the WCN

When you finalize the WCN, disposable materials are automatically converted to wastage.

  1. Click "Finalize WCN"

    On the completed collection order, click Finalize WCN.

  2. Review the Summary

    The finalization summary will show:

    • Regular materials ‚Üí Added to inventory
    • Composite materials ‚Üí Split into components
    • Disposable materials ‚Üí Marked for wastage (100%)

    Disposable Material Notice:

    "Oil Sludge (50 L) will be converted to wastage with type: Contamination"

  3. Confirm Finalization

    Click Confirm to finalize. The system will:

    • Create a wastage record with status Pending
    • Link the wastage to the collection order
    • Skip adding the material to inventory
    • Include the wastage cost in reports

Step 4: Verify the Results

After finalization, verify that the wastage was created correctly.

  1. Check Wastage Records

    Go to: Wastage page

    You should see a new wastage record with:

    Status Pending
    Material Contaminated Oil Mixture
    Quantity 50 liters (100% of collected)
    Waste Type Contamination
    Description Disposable material collected via WCN-XXXX
  2. Verify No Inventory Entry

    Go to: Inventory ‚Üí Stock Overview

    The disposable material should NOT appear in inventory, or should show 0 stock.

    This is expected!

    Disposable materials skip inventory entirely. They are tracked only as wastage records.

  3. Approve the Wastage

    A manager or admin must approve the wastage record:

    1. Open the wastage record
    2. Review the details
    3. Click Approve or Reject

    Once approved, the wastage is included in reports and analytics.

Troubleshooting

Issue Solution
Can't enable Disposable toggle Check if the material is marked as Composite. Composite materials cannot be disposable.
Wastage not created after WCN Verify the material has is_disposable = true in Material Master.
Wrong waste type on wastage Edit the material and change the Default Waste Type for future collections.
Material appeared in inventory The material was not marked as disposable before finalization. Mark it now for future collections.
Wastage shows 0 cost Set a Standard Price or Contract Rate for the material to track disposal costs.
