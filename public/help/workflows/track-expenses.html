Tracking Expenses

Learn how to submit, track, and manage expenses through the petty cash system. This guide covers both mobile portal submission and admin entry methods.

Who Can Submit Expenses

All Staff can submit via mobile portal. Accounts can submit via admin panel.

Two Ways to Submit Expenses

Mobile Portal (PC Users)

For staff members assigned as PC Users:

  • Submit from anywhere via phone
  • Quick QR + PIN access
  • Camera for receipt photos

Admin Panel

For Accounts Staff entering expenses:

  • Full system access
  • Can enter for any card
  • Assign to specific PC users

Submitting via Mobile Portal

PC Users can submit expenses on-the-go:

  1. Step 1: Access the Portal

    Open the portal by:

    • Scanning your QR code, OR
    • Opening the portal URL in your browser

    Enter your 4-digit PIN to log in.

  2. Step 2: Tap "Submit Expense"

    On the dashboard, tap the large Submit Expense button.

  3. Step 3: Enter Amount

    Type the expense amount using the numeric keypad.

  4. Step 4: Select Category

    Choose the appropriate category:

    • Transportation / Fuel
    • Office Supplies
    • Meals & Entertainment
    • Maintenance & Repairs
    • Utilities
    • Miscellaneous
  5. Step 5: Add Description

    Write a brief description explaining the expense (e.g., "Fuel for delivery vehicle").

  6. Step 6: Attach Receipt

    Tap Add Receipt and take a photo or select from gallery.

    Good Receipt Photos

    Ensure receipt is flat, well-lit, and all text is readable. Include date and amount.

  7. Step 7: Submit

    Review your entry and tap Submit. You'll see a confirmation.

Tracking Your Expense Status

For PC Users (Mobile Portal)

  1. Log into the mobile portal
  2. Tap My Expenses
  3. View list of all your submissions
  4. Tap any expense to see details

Expense Statuses

Status Meaning Action Needed
Pending Waiting for manager approval Wait for review
Approved Approved and processed None - complete
Rejected Rejected by manager Review reason, possibly resubmit

If Your Expense is Rejected

  1. View the rejection reason (tap to see details)
  2. Understand why it was rejected
  3. If correctable, submit a new expense with corrections
  4. If you disagree, speak with your manager

Best Practices

  • Submit immediately: Record expenses as they happen
  • Keep receipts: Always photograph before they fade or get lost
  • Be specific: Clear descriptions help faster approvals
  • Choose correct category: Helps accounting and reporting
  • Check balance: Verify card has sufficient funds before making purchases
  • Monitor status: Follow up if pending too long (>48 hours)
