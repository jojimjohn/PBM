Sales Staff Guide

As Sales Staff, you manage customer relationships, create sales orders, and generate invoices. This guide covers your daily tasks and best practices for sales operations.

Sales Staff Role

Your System Access

Full Access

  • Customer Management
  • Customer Contracts
  • Sales Orders
  • Invoices

View Only

  • Inventory levels
  • Material prices
  • Your sales reports

No Access

  • Purchase orders
  • Supplier management
  • System settings
  • User management

Your Daily Workflow

  1. Check Dashboard

    Start by reviewing:

    • Pending orders requiring action
    • Today's deliveries
    • Customer inquiries
  2. Process Customer Orders

    Create sales orders for customer requests. Check inventory before promising delivery dates.

  3. Generate Invoices

    Generate invoices for completed deliveries and send to customers.

  4. Update Customer Records

    Keep customer contact information current. Note any special requirements.

Key Tasks

Creating a Sales Order

  1. Go to Sales → Sales Orders
  2. Click + New Order
  3. Select customer and delivery location
  4. Add materials with quantities (contract rates apply automatically)
  5. Set delivery date and payment terms
  6. Submit the order

See Sales Module for detailed instructions.

Using Contract Rates

When creating orders for customers with active contracts:

  • Contract rates apply automatically when you select material
  • Green indicator = Contract rate is lower than market
  • Yellow warning = Contract expiring soon
  • Alert customer if their contract is about to expire

See Using Contract Rates for more details.

Managing Customers

  • Add new customers with complete contact information
  • Configure all delivery locations
  • Keep contact details updated
  • Monitor contract expiration dates

Sales Best Practices

  • Verify inventory: Always check stock availability before confirming orders
  • Confirm pricing: Double-check contract rates before quoting customers
  • Complete orders: Don't leave orders in draft status
  • Timely invoicing: Generate invoices immediately after delivery
  • Document everything: Add notes to orders for special instructions
  • Proactive communication: Notify customers of expiring contracts

Sales Staff Daily Checklist

  • ☐ Review pending sales orders
  • ☐ Check today's scheduled deliveries
  • ☐ Generate invoices for completed deliveries
  • ☐ Follow up on customer inquiries
  • ☐ Check for contracts expiring this month
  • ☐ Update any changed customer information
