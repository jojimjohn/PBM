Manager Guide

As a Manager, you oversee operations, approve requests, and monitor business performance. This guide covers your approval workflows and management responsibilities.

Manager Role

Your Responsibilities

Approvals

  • Approve/reject expenses
  • Approve wastage records
  • Review purchase orders
  • Validate collection orders

Oversight

  • Monitor team activities
  • Review sales performance
  • Track purchase costs
  • Ensure compliance

Reporting

  • Generate performance reports
  • Analyze trends
  • Track KPIs
  • Present to leadership

Decision Making

  • Contract negotiations
  • Pricing decisions
  • Resource allocation
  • Process improvements

Your Approval Queue

Your dashboard shows pending items requiring your attention:

Check Daily

Review your approval queue at least once daily. Pending items block staff from completing their work.

Types of Approvals

Item Type Submitted By What to Review
Expenses All staff via petty cash Amount, category, receipt, justification
Wastage Purchase staff Quantity, reason, supporting documentation
Purchase Orders Purchase staff Supplier, amounts, terms
PO Amendments Purchase staff Changes from original, justification

Processing Expense Approvals

  1. Access Pending Expenses

    Go to Petty Cash → Approvals tab, or click the notification badge.

  2. Review Each Expense

    Click to view details:

    • Is the amount reasonable?
    • Is the category appropriate?
    • Is the receipt attached and legible?
    • Does the description justify the expense?
  3. Approve or Reject
    • Approve: Click "Approve" - amount is deducted from card
    • Reject: Click "Reject" and provide a clear reason
Approval Best Practice

When rejecting, always provide a specific, actionable reason so the submitter can correct and resubmit if appropriate.

Processing Wastage Approvals

Wastage records require careful review as they affect inventory and profitability.

What to Check

  • Quantity: Is this a realistic amount for the reported incident?
  • Type: Does the wastage type match the description?
  • Timing: Was this reported promptly after the incident?
  • Pattern: Is this a recurring issue that needs investigation?
Important

Approved wastage permanently reduces inventory. Verify the accuracy before approving, especially for large amounts.

Weekly Review

Manager Daily Checklist

  • ☐ Review and process pending expense approvals
  • ☐ Review and process pending wastage approvals
  • ☐ Check sales order status and deliveries
  • ☐ Review purchase order status
  • ☐ Check low stock alerts
  • ☐ Review expiring contracts (within 30 days)
  • ☐ Address any escalated issues
